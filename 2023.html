Welcome!

Your mission:

To dispose of as much Dubble Bubble Gum as possible. The competition will take place from Monday, December 4th to Sunday, December 10.

If you want to submit your scores, send them to luckycharmbowl2022@gmail.com. If you do not wish your name to be displayed on the website, please request an alias in the email.

Here are the results from last year!

Some reasonable rules:

  • Scoring.
    • You will be scored on how long a piece of gum is in your mouth for.
    • The amount of points earned per minute decreases after fifteen minutes, according to the trajectory depicted here: https://www.desmos.com/calculator/i6gcjmricf
    • Any piece of gum in your mouth for less than ten minutes scores no points.
    • Submit the amount of minutes for each piece of gum at the end of each day.
  • Don't disturb the bubble, or we'll give you trouble!
    • Only one piece of gum counts at a time. You can have more than one in your mouth, but only the oldest one will score points.
    • If the gum exits your face without you knowing when, that piece of gum grants no points.
    • Chewing not required.
  • No Marshmallow Maties!
    • No counterfeit gum. Dubble Bubble™ only.
    • It is recommended that you source your gum from the Math Lounge.

For health reasons, this challenge is discouraged.
